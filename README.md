# GreenLeaf Bio Website — Frontend README

## Overview
This repository contains the frontend layout for the GreenLeaf Bio website.  
The site is designed as a **B2B lead-generation platform** with clear pathways for quote requests and direct inquiries.

---

## Site Structure (Pages)

### 1) Home / Landing Page
**Purpose:** Primary entry point that communicates:
- Premium compostable packaging for businesses
- Trust / compliance positioning
- Clear CTAs (Request a Quote, Contact)

**Key Sections:**
- Hero + primary CTA(s)
- Quick product overview (Phase 1)
- Industries served (optional section)
- Certifications preview
- Trust / credibility highlights
- Footer with contact options

---

### 2) Products
**Purpose:** Showcase Phase 1 product categories with inquiry-based CTAs (no checkout).

**Recommended Layout:**
- Product category cards/sections
- Each category: short description + use case + CTA (“Request a Quote”)
- Optional: product detail sections (if content is provided)

---

### 3) Certifications & Compliance
**Purpose:** Build trust with institutional buyers and ensure compliance messaging is clear.

**Recommended Layout:**
- Standards overview (ASTM / EN, municipal compatibility, etc.)
- Certification badges / proof points (only what is verified)
- FAQ or disclaimer area for careful claims

---

### 4) About Us
**Purpose:** Establish credibility and long-term supply reliability.

**Recommended Layout:**
- Company overview & mission
- Manufacturing capability / scalability messaging
- Service regions (Canada + USA)
- Sustainability positioning (professional, no “greenwashing”)

---

### 5) Contact
**Purpose:** Convert visitors into leads.

**Contact Options (to include):**
- Contact form (lead capture)
- Business email: info@greenleaf-bio.com
- Phone number
- WhatsApp contact

**Recommended Fields (Form):**
- Name
- Company
- Email
- Phone (optional)
- Product interest (dropdown)
- Estimated volume / frequency (optional)
- Message

---

## Navigation
Primary nav:
- Home
- Products
- Certifications & Compliance
- About Us
- Contact
- CTA Button: **Get a Quote**

---

## Notes
- Website should remain **informational + inquiry-based** (no ecommerce checkout).
- Product and lifestyle imagery can be provided by client or generated/outsourced.
- SEO setup and on-page optimization will be included in initial phase.
